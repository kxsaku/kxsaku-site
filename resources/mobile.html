Client Terms & Policies

Terms Overview

Last updated:

1) Scope of Work Work is performed against an agreed scope. If scope changes midstream, SNS confirms the change before continuing so time and cost stay predictable.
2) Scheduling & Deposits Some issues only become visible on-site. For certain projects or equipment procurement, a deposit may be required to reserve time and/or purchase hardware.
3) Payments (One-Time Work) One-time work is invoiced after completion unless otherwise agreed. If an invoice becomes overdue, ongoing support may pause until resolved.
4) Subscription Support Subscription support is billed on the schedule shown at checkout. If payment fails or becomes past due, service can be paused until brought current.
5) Access & Ownership Client-owned systems remain client-owned. If SNS is granted admin access, it's for performing the agreed work. Access can be reduced or removed after the job.
6) Data Handling & Privacy SNS minimizes data collection. Information submitted is used to respond, schedule, and deliver service. Technical details may be retained for continuity.
7) Third-Party Services Some outcomes depend on third parties (ISP, vendor portals, payment processors). SNS can configure and harden, but cannot guarantee third-party availability.
8) Acceptable Use Do not submit false information, impersonate others, or attempt unauthorized access. SNS may restrict access to protect systems and service quality.
9) Service Limitations Networking work involves variables outside any one party's control. SNS focuses on measurable improvement, but cannot promise "perfect Wi-Fi everywhere."
10) Changes to Terms SNS may update these terms to reflect product changes, policy improvements, or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.
