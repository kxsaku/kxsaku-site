FAQ

Quick answers

Do you always require an assessment first? ›

Most of the time, yes. It prevents wasted spend and makes the fix precise. If the job is clearly defined (like a simple equipment swap), we can skip it.

Will you force a full hardware upgrade? ›

No. We troubleshoot and tune first. Hardware recommendations are only made when they’re the real bottleneck.

Can you work after hours or weekends? ›

Yes, depending on scope. Use the inquiry form and note your preferred windows.

What brands do you support? ›

Whatever fits the environment and budget. The priority is stability and manageability, not brand hype.
