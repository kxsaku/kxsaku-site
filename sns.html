HOW A PROJECT WORKS

From idea to working network

1. Discovery & goals (email)

You email me a quick overview: what your business does, how many people you have, your current internet connection, and what’s not working today.

2. Design & quote

I propose a network layout and recommended hardware (rack, router, switches, APs, etc.). Pricing is project-based and quote-only so small offices aren’t paying enterprise rates.

3. Procurement & install

With your approval, I order the agreed equipment. I bring and install what fits in my vehicle; larger items can ship directly to your office. I don’t handle heavy construction or forklift moves.

4. Configuration & handover

I configure the network, connect to your ISP, verify key workstations, and provide simple documentation so you know what’s installed and where.

Note: I assume you already have an internet provider. If you don’t, I can advise on what to ask for when ordering service.

ONGOING NETWORK CARE

Optional monthly subscription

After the initial build-out, you can keep me on as your part-time network admin. Instead of hiring full-time IT, you get a dedicated person who already knows your environment.

  • Firmware/OS updates for agreed network devices.
  • Configuration changes as you grow: new users, VLANs, ports, Wi-Fi tweaks.
  • Remote troubleshooting for network-related issues during agreed hours.
  • Upgrade planning when you start to outgrow the original design.

Monthly pricing is custom and depends on devices under management, number of locations, and expected ticket volume. All support starts via email and will move into a proper ticketing system as the service grows.
